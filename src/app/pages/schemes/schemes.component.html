Fixed Deposit: Secure your money, secure your future

Fixed Deposits offer guaranteed returns and are a great way to lock in your funds for a specific duration at higher interest rates.

A Fixed Deposit (FD) is a secure and reliable way to grow your savings. With guaranteed returns and competitive interest rates, it’s the perfect option for achieving your financial goals.

Key Features
